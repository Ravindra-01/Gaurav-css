Admission Overview

Cluny Convent School, Ranchi welcomes applications from students who wish to grow academically, morally, and spiritually. Admissions are granted strictly on merit and availability of seats as per school norms.

Admission Process

  • Admission forms are available at the school office.
  • Forms must be filled and submitted before the due date.
  • Students may be required to appear for an entrance test.
  • Interaction with parents and students will be conducted.
  • Final admission is subject to availability of seats.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Minimum age 3+ years.
  • Class I onwards: Based on previous class performance.
  • Transfer Certificate is mandatory for Class II onwards.
  • Students must meet academic and age requirements.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Passport Size Photographs (2)

Fee Structure

The fee structure is affordable and designed to provide quality education. Detailed fee information can be collected from the school office during working hours.
